FAQ’S

  1. How do I pay monthly installment?

Ans: Monthly installment can be paid in Cash @ djm / Card / Transfer to DJM account and update via DJMJEWELS.COM with transaction snapshot.

  1. What If I Discontinue?

Ans: Members who wish to discontinue and close the card before the maturity, they will not be eligible for “No Wastage” benefit on their purchase, and they will be charged the usual wastage.

  1. What if I don’t pay continuously (Irregular)?

Ans: The Schemes Maturity date will be postponed depending upon their payment pattern.

  1. How much beneficial is the scheme for me?

Ans: DJMS ‘BB’-Scheme helps you to plan in advance to buy Jewellery for Wedding, Festivals, Akshaya Tritiya, and Birthdays. Hence it is 0% wastage scheme you will getting more Jewellery than you have expected.

  1. Will wastage (VA) will be charged on special items Diamond, Precious stone and other special Jewellery?

Ans: Yes, wastage will be charged extra, for some special item like Diamond, Precious stone & other special item which attract wastage of 14% and above.

  1. Will there be VAT?

Ans: VAT and other Govt. Levies if any will be borne by member at the time of purchase.

  1. What Gold Price will be charged for the scheme?

Ans: Prices that prevail on the date of your purchase will be charged.

  1. Can I buy Gold Coins in this Scheme?

Ans: Yes, You can buy Gold Coins in this scheme.

  1. Will wastage be charged for the extra Gold I purchase that my scheme amount?

Ans: Yes, wastage will be charged for the extra Gold purchased then your scheme amount.

  1. Can I Get the Cash back in this scheme?

Ans: No, Cash won’t be given other than Jewells or Coins.

  1. By WHAT DATE should I pay every month?

Ans: You can pay on any date, but once in a month you should pay.
